c. Problem Statement
Every day, 40% of food produced in India is wasted — mostly by restaurants, hotels, and catering businesses — while 189 million people go to sleep hungry.

There is no efficient, accountable, real-time system to bridge this gap. Existing solutions rely on phone calls, manual coordination and have no delivery accountability.

The result: edible food rots in kitchens while people starve within kilometers.

Brief About Our Solution
ZeroWaste is a multi-role real-time web platform that digitises the full food donation lifecycle — from a restaurant posting surplus food, to an NGO claiming it, to a volunteer delivering it with OTP-verified accountability.
🍱
Donor Portal
Restaurants, hotels and caterers post surplus food listings with geo-location, expiry time and food details. Auto-fill address from registered profile or live GPS.
🏢
NGO Dashboard
NGOs browse and instantly claim listings. Real-time live tracker shows volunteer status, ETA, and auto-generates a delivery OTP when food is picked up.
🏍️
Volunteer App
Volunteers see available tasks sorted by distance. Accept tasks, view route map, verify pickup via donor OTP, confirm delivery via NGO OTP — and earn automatically.
💡 Key innovation: OTP-gated verification at both pickup and delivery creates an auditable, tamper-proof chain of custody. Wallet payouts are automatic on confirmed delivery — no manual processing.

Opportunities & USP
a. How is it different?
  • vs. Zomato/Swiggy: Purely commercial — no mechanism for food donation at all.
  • vs. Manual drives (Robin Hood Army): Phone/WhatsApp based. No real-time tracking, no accountability, no automated payouts.
  • vs. Food banks: Static drop-off model. No last-mile delivery. Relies on donors to physically travel.
  • ZeroWaste: End-to-end digital pipeline. Auto-assigns nearest volunteer. OTP-verified. Real-time for all 3 parties simultaneously.
b. How does it solve the problem?
  • Reduces friction from minutes of calls to one click.
  • Auto-matches volunteers by proximity — food stays fresh.
  • OTP chain prevents false delivery claims.
  • Financial incentive keeps volunteer supply healthy.

Features Offered
📍
Geo-Location Food Posting
Donors post listings with live GPS or registered address. Distance shown to volunteers in real-time.
⚡
Auto Volunteer Assignment
System auto-finds the nearest available volunteer when an NGO claims a listing.
🔐
Dual OTP Verification
Pickup OTP → donor. Delivery OTP → NGO. Volunteer must enter both to advance status.
📡
Real-time NGO Tracker
Live updates via Socket.IO: volunteer assigned → picked up → en route → delivered. With ETA.
💳
Wallet System
NGO pre-funded wallet. On delivery, payout auto-debits NGO and credits volunteer. Full audit trail.
🗺️
Route Map + ETA
Interactive OpenStreetMap with pickup/drop-off pins. Distance and ETA calculated (Haversine).
🤖
AI Demand Forecasting
Predicts surplus food days, meal estimates, and peak times from historical listing data.
🔔
Real-time Notifications
In-app notification bell with socket events. OTP popup appears on any dashboard page automatically.
📊
Role-based Dashboards
Fully separate UX for Donor, NGO and Volunteer with analytics, earnings, history and stats.
